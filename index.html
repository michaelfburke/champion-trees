Liner notes: Over the past three years we were willing shiners waiting, or at least we were trying to be so. We were trying to lie really straight and flat and still and be like reeds for the wind to fill with music. But then the wind wouldn't blow for days or we would get restless and walk around at precisely the moment it started blowing again. Sometimes it was really fun. Sometimes it was like collaborating on a 1000 piece puzzle of the sky via email. All of the time it felt kind of worthwhile. Eventually we saw the various strands we had and we gathered them together and we called the whole thing 'A Duck's Water Off My Back'. credits released November 20, 2025 All songs by Champion Trees Mix & master by André Hough Cover art adapted from 'Shape of a Duck' by Jayshal Gajjar Lyrics, vocals, guitars by Francis Christie Bass, backing vocals by Lex Pienaar Violin, synthesizers by James Bickford-Smith Drums by Langa Dubazana Saxophone by Michael Burke Clarinet by Michelle Hromin Cello by Alex Watsham
