► ABOUT THE BAND ◄

Champion Trees are a band born in the wild — raw, rootsy, and reaching toward the light. Like the ancient trees they're named after, their music stands tall, endures, and shelters all who listen.

From misty mountains to basement shows, Champion Trees bring an untamed sound that blends folk, rock, and something that can only be described as "sonic bark and branch."

Their music is available now on Bandcamp. Support independent music. Buy their stuff.
